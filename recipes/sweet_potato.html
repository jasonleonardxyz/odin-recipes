Air Fryer Sweet Potato Chips

Description

A light and healthy alternative to processed potato chips. Quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 large (8 ounce) sweet potato
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Slice potato into 1/16-inch-thick rounds using a mandoline. Put potato slices in a large bowl of cold water. Soak for about 20 minutes. Drain and pat dry with paper towels. Wipe out the bowl.
  2. Return potato slices to bowl. Add oil, salt and pepper and toss.
  3. Preheat air fryer to 180 degrees C. Lightly coat air fryer baset with cooking spray. Working in batches if needed, arrange potato slices in an even layer in the basket. Do not overcrowd.
  4. Cook until golden and crispy, about 12 to 16 minutes. Turn and rearrange every 4 minutes. Watch closely in the last 3 minutes of cooking. Check every 30 seconds and use tongs to remove finished chips.
  5. Let chips cool for 5 minutes. Can be stored in airtight container up to 3 days.
