Chicken Murphy

Description

Chicken Murphy is a stew made with Italian sausage, chicken thighs, potatoes, mushrooms, and peppers in a garlic and white wine sauce. The dish is italian inspired and can be cooked in one pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1 inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tabelspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound cremini mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 pound russet potatoes, scrubbed and cubed
  • 1 cup dry white whine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup brine from Peppadew peppers
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup Peppadew peppers, halved
  • chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Gather all of the ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage in a 5-to-6 quart Dutch oven over medium heat until browned, 6 to 8 minutes; transfer to a bowl.
  3. Place flour in shallow plate. Dredge chicken in flour to coat.
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in Dutch oven; add chicken and cook until lightly browned, 4 to 5 minutes. Transfer chicken to bowl with sausage.
  5. Add remaining oil to Dutch oven. Add mushrooms, onion, and bell peppers. Cook until tender.
  6. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant.
  7. Stir in potatoes, white wine, chicken broth, brine, Italian seasoning, and salt; bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and allow to simmer until potatoes are tender.
  8. Add Peppadew peppers and browned chicken and sausage; cook and stir until heated through and sauce is slightly thickened, about 3 minutes.
  9. Sprinkle with parsley before serving.
